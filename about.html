Career Path

1. Formation and Exploration (2006–2014)

Early career: working with facade materials, cottages, small-scale civic architecture. Gained practical technical skills and began initial design work. Based in Kazan, combined with academic studies.

2. Professional Development (2014–2016)

Transition to more structured architectural practice (Zhilstroyservice, Kontinent Group). Experience with reconstruction, documentation, and standardization. Strengthening of core project competencies: ESKD, SPDS, and phased design processes.

3. International and Team Experience (2016–2022)

Work at Chapman Taylor on international projects. Team leadership, BIM workflows, involvement in residential and commercial design. Geographic scope: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Uzbekistan. Progression to a senior architect role.

4. Deepening Architectural and Visual Practice (2022–2025)

Projects at APEX and UNK. Focused on large residential complexes. Increased involvement in visual concepts and presentation strategies.

5. Transition Toward Visualization and Independent Practice (2025–...)

Focus on architectural visualization. Development of an individual visual language and remote practice. Starting to build a personal brand and portfolio as an independent architectural author.
