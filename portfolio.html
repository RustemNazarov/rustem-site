Selected Works

Mirabad Residential Complex
Concept development for a mixed-use residential complex in Uzbekistan, featuring traditional design elements. The project involved creating a harmonious blend of modern living spaces with regional architectural motifs, while addressing urban density challenges and providing communal areas that reflect local cultural practices.
Riyadh Boulevard Office Tower
Design development for a commercial high-rise in Saudi Arabia, focusing on innovative facade solutions. The tower incorporates advanced sun-shading techniques and energy-efficient glazing systems adapted to the local climate, while maintaining a distinctive architectural identity within the business district.
Dubai Mall Cinema Complex
Architectural concept and visualization for a luxury cinema complex in UAE, emphasizing interior spatial quality and premium visitor experience. The design process included developing unique thematic zones within the complex, optimizing circulation flows for high visitor numbers, and integrating cutting-edge audiovisual technologies into the architectural framework.
