Selected Work

Mixed-Use Complex: Retail, Entertainment & Offices, Pokrovskoye-Streshnevo, Moscow

As architectural lead, I developed the concept for this 45,000m² mixed-use complex, creating a cohesive identity across retail, entertainment and office functions.

Concept Development
  • Developed architectural concept responding to historic context
  • Created distinctive facade identity with rhythmic vertical fins
  • Established material palette with terracotta panels and glass
Functional Zoning
  • Organized complex into three distinct functional blocks
  • Designed seamless pedestrian flows between retail and entertainment zones
  • Created visual connections through strategic openings
Façade Engineering
  • Developed modular unitized curtain wall system
  • Integrated vertical shading fins with structural integrity
  • Coordinated complex interfaces between different facade systems
Public Realm
  • Designed multi-level public plaza with amphitheater seating
  • Created signature canopy structures for weather protection
  • Developed comprehensive landscape integration strategy
Technical Coordination
  • Led multi-disciplinary coordination across 8 engineering teams
  • Developed integrated BIM model for clash detection
  • Implemented sustainable design principles (BREEAM Very Good)
