Mixed-Use Tower Complex (136m)

As conceptual lead for this 136m mixed-use development, I shaped the architectural identity through comprehensive design solutions across all project phases.

Facade Design
  • Developed modular and stick curtain wall systems with structural glazing
  • Created rhythmic vertical louvre patterns to visually lighten tower massing
  • Established material hierarchy with glass dominance and aluminum accents
Volumetric Composition
  • Designed sculptural setbacks at Levels 5 and 18 to break verticality
  • Integrated terraces as transitional spaces between public and private zones
  • Balanced podium transparency with tower solidity
Lighting Strategy
  • Conceptualized contour lighting for facade elements with dynamic white tuning
  • Designed signature cantilevered glass canopies as luminous gateways
  • Developed scenario-based lighting modes for daily and special events
Visual Coordination
  • Created tenant signage guidelines preserving architectural clarity
  • Established material continuity between towers and podium
  • Supervised visualization of key spaces including atriums and terraces
Technical Integration
  • Implemented high-rated safety glazing for full-height windows
  • Coordinated fire safety solutions for 32-story tower
  • Developed universal design principles for accessibility
  • Resolved complex interface details between curtain walls and terraces
